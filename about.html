Our Story

Founded in 2014, NHPP Investments CC was established to meet the growing need for dependable, diversified procurement services across Namibia. What began as a small operation focused on waste bins and stationery has evolved into a multi-sector supplier serving clients in healthcare, safety, ICT, hospitality, and government.

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with suppliers and clients alike, earning a reputation for reliability, quality, and competitive pricing. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted partner for institutions across Namibia.

Today, NHPP Investments CC continues to grow and adapt to meet the evolving needs of our clients, always maintaining our core values of integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction.
